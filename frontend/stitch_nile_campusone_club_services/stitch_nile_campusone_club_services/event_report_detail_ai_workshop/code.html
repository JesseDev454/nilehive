Execution Narrative

The 'Intro to AI Workshop' was executed smoothly in Hall B4. Setup began an hour prior, ensuring all power strips and campus Wi-Fi extensions were operational, which proved critical given the heavy laptop usage.

Successes

Engagement was notably high. The transition from theoretical ethics to hands-on Python notebooks via Google Colab kept the momentum strong. The pizza break at the 60-minute mark successfully prevented drop-off before the final coding sprint.

Challenges & Learnings

We experienced minor delays getting everyone authenticated onto the campus guest network. Future technical workshops should include a pre-event email with environment setup instructions to save the first 15 minutes. Additionally, 10 more chairs were needed than anticipated.
