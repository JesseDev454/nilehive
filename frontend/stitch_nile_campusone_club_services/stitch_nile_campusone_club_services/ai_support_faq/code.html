How do I join a club?

smart_toy

Joining a club is an easy quest! Here is what you need to do:

  1. Navigate to the Student Hub using the menu on the left.
  2. Browse the directory of active organizations.
  3. When you find a club that matches your interests, click their card to view details.
  4. Hit the Request to Join button.

The club President will review your request shortly. Need help finding a specific club?
